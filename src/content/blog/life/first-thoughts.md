This is a sample life blog post. Use this section to share your thoughts, reflections, and everyday moments.

## What Goes Here

This is your space for:

- Personal reflections and musings
- Life updates and milestones
- Random thoughts and observations
- Things you've learned
- Stories and experiences

## How to Add New Posts

1. Create a new `.md` file in `src/content/blog/life/`
2. Add the frontmatter at the top (title, description, date, image, tags)
3. Write your content in markdown below the frontmatter
4. The post will automatically appear on the Life & Thoughts section

Feel free to write whatever's on your mind!
