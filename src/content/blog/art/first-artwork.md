## Sharing Your Art

Each post can include:

- A featured image at the top
- Detailed descriptions of your process
- Multiple images throughout the post
- Tags to categorize your work

## The Creative Process

Write about your inspiration, techniques, and the story behind each piece. Markdown makes it easy to format your content with headers, lists, and more.
